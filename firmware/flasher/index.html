HC33 BLE Proxy installer

Flashes the BLE proxy firmware onto an HC33 (Heltec HT-HC33). Plug the HC33 into your computer with a USB-C cable, fill out the form, and click Install.

First time? Read this.
  • You need a Chromium-based browser — Chrome, Edge, Brave, Arc, Opera. Firefox and Safari don't support Web Serial.
  • On Windows you also need the SiLabs CP210x driver. Mac and Linux usually have it built in.
  • Re-running this installer is the way to change config — just fill the form again and re-flash. The MM6108 calibration data (key_data partition) is preserved.
